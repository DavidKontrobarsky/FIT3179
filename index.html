A Complete History of Political Protests (1990 - 2018)

A look at the causes and outcomes of protests

What affects the outcome of political protests? This visualisation looks at multiple dimensions of political protests including location, number of protesters and duration of protests to identify some trends in how states react to mass mobilisation of its citizens. Here's what I found.

Firstly we can look at the geographical location of protests around the world as well as their participant numbers and duration. There is a clear protest hotspot in Europe but particularly around Eastern Europe where some long lasting protests occured.
